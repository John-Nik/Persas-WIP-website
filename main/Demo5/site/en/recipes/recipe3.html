Healthy Melomakarona

Ingredients:

  • 1100 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 300 grams of olive oil
  • 300 grams of sunflower oil
  • 50 grams of cognac
  • 160 grams of orange juice
  • 20 grams of stevia sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of soda
  • 1 teaspoon of trimmed cloves
  • 2 teaspoons of cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • small pieces of coconuts
  • 2 trimmed down orange bark
    for the syrup:
  • 250 grams of agave syrup
  • a trimmed mushroom
  • 1 piece of cinnamon
  • 2 cloves pieces
  • 100 grams of orange or mushroom juice
  • 100 grams of water

    or

  • 300 grams of honey
  • 200 ml of water
  • 2 cinammon sticks
  • 1 lemon's juice

How to cook:

Melomakarono cooking:

We pour in a big bowl the olive oil, sunflower oil, stevia sugar, cognac, cinammon, the trimmed clove and the trimmed orange bark and we mix until the mixture is uniform.

We mix the soda with the orange juice in a separate container, and the flour with baking powder in a separate container aswell. We empty the mixture of orange juice and soda into the big bowl we prepared beforehand, and while mixing all of the ingredients together we start slowly pouring in the contents of the flour and baking soda, all without stopping the mixing. We do this until the mixture looks uniform.

We will check if the mixture is ready. In order to do that, we will take a very small sample and try to shape up a melomakarono. If the result is an oily melomakarono, then well done! You've completed the mixing job.

We finish up by shaping them all into the desired size and shape and place them in a pre-heated oven (around 160°C - air setting) for close to 25 minutes.

Once they start getting the golden yellow color, we take them out of the oven. We will start cooking up the cyrups when the melomakarona temperature starts to dial down to room-temperature.

Syrup Cooking:

We place all the syrup's ingredients inside a pan and we warm the pan up for 3-5 minutes. Afterwards, we take off the pan from the over's eye or flame, we lower the flame to 1/3 of its original power and place the pan back, with the idea we'll maintain the syrup in a warm but not too hot temperature.

To finish, we dip the melomakarona in the warm syrup for 10-20 seconds in each side, and they're ready to be served.

Enjoy your Melomakarona!
