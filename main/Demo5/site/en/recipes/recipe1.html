Healthy Chocolate Bar Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of plums
  • 1 cup of almond nuts (or any other nuts you prefer)
  • 3/4 of a cup of betel nuts
  • 2 spoons of cocoa soup (not sweetened)
  • a pinch of salt

How to cook:

Pour all the ingredients in a blender and blend it all at a high speed until they have a smooth, thick texture.

Put the blended result ina plate and try to shape it into an easy-to-cut later shape.

Cover it and store it in the fridge for about 30 minutes.

When you take it out, cut it in your desired servings with a sharp knife.

You can later cover it up and store the remaining pieces in a fridge. (They can be preserved for up to a week)

Enjoy your Chocy Snack!
